What is GPNJ?

Get Plugged was built on a simple belief: the best moments are designed. The team connects emerging talent with real audiences through curated lineups, tight production, and clear communication. With every event we intend to build on artist-first practices and partnerships with DJs, creators, and vendors. Every show is mapped for flow, tuned for sound, and captured with content that extends the life of the night. From intimate rooms to high-energy pop-ups, community thrives and memories stick.

Our Mission: Connecting creatives and audiences through events that foster an intimate and up-lifting environment.
